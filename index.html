My accomplishments

I completed numerous tasks during my career, here is a short list of the ones I'm the most proud of.

I industrialized unique tires

I have successfully industrialized tires used during international races such as Dakar, Baja Score or The Mint 400. To support the most extreme conditions, I introduced new technologies aiming to improve racing performances.


I created multiple interactive dasboards

The dashboards are using the Dash/Plotly framework and are getting data from various sources such as SQL tables or Webref. Those Online dashboards aim to help internal customers better apprehend and understand available data.


I designed a 17 feet mower with an endwise transport system

This mower sold worldwide is used by farmers looking for a high cutting width (17 feet) with a low horsepower tractor.
Our team faced numerous challenges that we succesfully addressed: define an endwise transport system, define a gearbox and driveline system to power the cutter-bar, or optimize the structure that supports the high stress a big structure can generate.


I defined the architecture of a new in-line wrapper

I started this project from scratch and made sure stakeholders from all departments were involved. For this highly complex project, we needed expertise for areas inculding hydraulic manifolds, road regulations, electronic controllers, patent compliance and utilization of a gaz engine.


I used various simulation tools

I used multiple analysis tools to confirm designs and reducce costly prototypes and tests. I had the opportunity to use FEA to calculate stress in complex structures, use kinematic analysis to check dynamic behavior and modal analysis to find natural frequencies.


I coded a program that predicts the cost of sheet-metal parts

I used here my newly acquired skills in data-science. The program extracts information from a DXF file representing a sheet-metal part in flat position and predicts its production cost without the involvement of manufacturing engineering.
