My accomplishments

I completed numerous tasks during my career and personnal life, here is a short list of the ones I'm the most proud of.

I coded a program that predicts the cost of sheet-metal parts

The program extracts information from a DXF file representing a sheet-metal part in flat position and predicts its production cost without the involvement of manufacturing engineering.

  • Report

I used deep learning technics to predict dog's breed from a picture

Sort pictures manually can be a tedious job. I created a program that can differentiate dogs from 120 breeds. I tested frameworks such as Keras/tensorflow and FastAI/Pytorch that provided an impressive accuracy of 85% breed recognition with the kernel resnet50.


I used Natural langage processing to classify text posted by user on Stackoverflow

Forums receive thousands of posts every hour and must be properly tagged to simplify future searches. I used Natural language processing and un-supervised methods (LDA and NMF) to recommend tags for user's posts.


I segmented customer behavior

Understanding customer's needs is the key for any business. To predict customer's profile I used ensemblist methods such as XGBoost or LightGBM to quiclky identify buying habits. This tool can be used for targeted marketting.


I used un-supervised technics to create a movie recommendation system

I used the IMDB database to recommend movies based on previous screening. I applied reduction dimension technics such as PCA and compared clustering results from KMeans and DBScan. Data cleaning and feature engineering where key to ensure good movie match.


I created a GUI that manages large amounts of pictures

The program based on Python, SQLite and PyQT allows any user to easilly store and find pictures. More recently I created another program that handles spectra based on TKInter.

I compete on different Kaggle competitions to gain additional experience in data science, to discuss data science with others and obviously to have fun! So far I participated to the House Prices and Santander competitions.
